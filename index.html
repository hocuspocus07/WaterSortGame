How to Play Water Sort

                    
Objective:
 Fill all tubes with a single color each.
                    
                    
Gameplay:

                    1. Click on an empty tube to select it.
                    2. Click on another tube to move colors from the selected tube to the destination tube.
                    3. Colors can only be moved if the destination tube is empty or has the same color at its top.
                    4. Use consecutive colors to optimize moves.
                    5. Click "Undo" to reverse the last move.
                    6. Win by filling all tubes with one color each.
                    
                    
Controls:

                    - Click: Select or move colors between tubes.
                    - Undo Button: Reverse the last move.
